Coastal Ice Ocean Prediction System (CIOPS) - EAST

The CIOPS-EAST model is supported by ECCC as an experimental system.

Variables from CIOPS-E available on the Ocean Navigator: water salinity (psu)
water temperature (°C)
water X Velocity (m/s)
water Y Velocity (m/s)
derived Critical depth (m)
derived deep sound channel axis (m)
derived sonic layer depth (m)
derived speed of sound (m/s)

Run cycle: Pseudo-analysis
Geographical Coverage: partial coverage within Canadian zone of North Atlantic Ocean
Spatial Resolution: 1/36 ° latitude and longitude
Vertical Coverage: 75 levels ranging from 1 m to 205 m thickness at depth
Temporal Resolution: Daily mean for all fields, and hourly surface fields
Temporal Coverage: From 2020-21-2020 to present
Data Source: Environment and Climate Change Canada
